About Rajaansh Industries

35 Years of Group Excellence in Woven Packaging

Rajaansh Industries is the woven packaging arm of a group with 35+ years of manufacturing heritage — built on precision, quality and trust.

Our Story

Deep Industry Roots. Purpose-Built for Scale.

Rajaansh Industries Pvt. Ltd. is the woven packaging business of a group with over 35 years of experience in manufacturing and industrial supply across India. Drawing on decades of operational know-how, Rajaansh was established with a single mission: manufacture world-class PP and HDPE woven packaging from an integrated Bangalore facility.

By integrating every step of the manufacturing process under one roof — from PP tape extrusion to circular weaving, lamination, printing and bag conversion — Rajaansh delivers the quality consistency that large-volume buyers in cement, fertilizer, sugar and food processing demand.

The company operates from an integrated facility in Bangalore, supplying cement majors, fertilizer producers, sugar mills, rice exporters and FIBC users across India and for export.

Our Capabilities

Integrated Under One Roof

Every process from raw PP granule to finished bag happens in our Bangalore facility — no outsourcing, no compromise on quality.

⚙️

Tape Extrusion

Modern tape extrusion lines producing PP and HDPE tapes from virgin resin to precise denier and tensile specifications.

🌀

Circular Weaving

Fleet of 32+ circular looms producing tubular fabric from 30cm to 100cm diameter at controlled weave count and GSM.

🔲

Lamination

In-line PP and BOPP lamination applying moisture-barrier film on one or both sides of fabric to precise micron thickness.

🎨

Flexographic Printing

Multi-colour flexo printing up to 8 colours with Pantone matching, registered print and rotogravure quality reproduction.

✂️

Bag Conversion

Cutting, stitching, gusseting, liner insertion, handle attachment and closure operations — all in-house with automated lines.

🔬

Quality Lab

In-house testing for GSM, denier, tensile strength, elongation, print adhesion, drop test and UV resistance at every stage.

Our Quality Promise

ISO 9001:2015 certification is not just a plaque on the wall. It represents a documented, audited quality management system that governs every decision at Rajaansh — from incoming raw material inspection to final shipment.

We refuse to compromise on raw material specification. All PP and HDPE used in production is virgin resin from approved suppliers — no recycled content in food-grade or structural applications.

Every consignment leaves with a full test report. Third-party inspection by SGS, Bureau Veritas or client-nominated agencies is welcomed.
