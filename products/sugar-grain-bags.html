Food Safety

Designed for Direct Food Contact

Food grain packaging demands more than mechanical performance — it must protect product purity and meet regulatory requirements throughout the supply chain.

Virgin PP Resin Only

We use only virgin (non-recycled) polypropylene for all food-grade bags. No contamination risk from recycled content in fabric touching food.

FSSAI Compliant Liner

HDPE/LDPE inner liners made from food-grade approved resin per FSSAI guidelines. Prevents moisture and contamination from woven fabric contact.

Food-Safe Printing Inks

All printing inks used on food packaging are non-toxic, heavy-metal free and comply with relevant food packaging contact material standards.

Tamper Evidence

Double-stitched top seam and heat-sealed liner options. Tamper-evident label zones and hologram attachment areas available on request.

Moisture Resistance

PP lamination or BOPP lamination creates a moisture barrier critical for sugar and grain during transportation and warehousing in humid conditions.

Ventilated Options

Open-weave ventilated fabric allows airflow for products like onion, potato and certain grains where breathability prevents condensation and spoilage.
