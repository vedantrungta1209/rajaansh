Why BOPP

The Premium Packaging Choice for Food Brands

BOPP lamination transforms a functional woven bag into a high-impact retail package that stands out on shelf and protects product quality.

✨

High-Gloss Surface

Mirror-like gloss finish that makes colours pop on retail shelves. Premium look at economical woven bag pricing.

💧

Moisture Barrier

BOPP film seals the woven fabric and prevents moisture ingress — critical for food products and longer shelf life.

🎨

Vibrant 8-Colour Print

Rotogravure printing with Pantone colour matching. Photo-realistic imagery, QR codes and barcodes all printable.

💪

Woven Strength Base

PP woven fabric core gives BOPP bags far superior puncture and tear resistance vs plain BOPP film packaging.

📦

Block Bottom Option

Block-bottom (square bottom) bags stand upright on shelf without leaning — dramatically better retail presence.

♻️

Recyclable

100% polypropylene — fully recyclable. Meets EPR (Extended Producer Responsibility) compliance requirements.
