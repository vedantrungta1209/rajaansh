Our Process

From Raw Material to Finished Product

A quality-driven manufacturing process ensures consistency, strength, and precision in every batch we produce.

1

Raw Material Sourcing

Virgin-grade PP/HDPE granules sourced from certified suppliers with strict material testing.

2

Tape Extrusion & Weaving

Granules extruded into flat tapes, stretched, and woven on circular looms at high speed.

3

Lamination & Printing

BOPP lamination for moisture resistance; multi-color printing using food-safe inks.

4

QC & Dispatch

Every batch undergoes load testing, dimension checks, and visual inspection before dispatch.
