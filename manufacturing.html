Our Manufacturing

Integrated. Controlled. Precise.

our integrated Bangalore facility produces everything from raw PP granule to finished printed bag — in-house.

Process Flow

6 Integrated Manufacturing Stages

Complete vertical integration means every variable is controlled — raw material grade, tape denier, weave density, lamination thickness, print register, and final bag dimensions.

1

Raw Material & Extrusion

Virgin PP and HDPE granules from approved polymer suppliers are processed through high-speed tape extruders. Molten polymer is extruded through flat die, quenched, slit and drawn into thin flat tapes of precise denier (800–2,000 denier) and width.

Virgin PP / HDPE only Denier controlled ±5% UV stabiliser additive
2

Circular Weaving

PP tapes are woven into tubular fabric on our fleet of 32+ circular looms at controlled weave count (8×8 to 14×14 strands/inch). Fabric weight is set by loom parameters — the foundation of every bag's mechanical performance.

32+ circular looms 40–120 GSM range GSM ±3% tolerance
3

Lamination

Woven fabric passes through lamination stations where PP or BOPP film is applied via extrusion coating or adhesive lamination to one or both fabric faces. Lamination creates the moisture barrier and the print substrate.

PP / BOPP film 20–30 micron film One side / both sides
4

Flexographic Printing

Multi-colour flexo printing on laminated fabric surface using food-safe inks. Up to 8 colours with Pantone matching, registration accuracy, photo-quality halftone and full bleed capability for demanding brand guidelines.

Up to 8 colours Pantone matched Food-safe inks
5

Bag Conversion

Printed fabric is cut, gusseted, stitched (or heat-sealed), fitted with handles, liners and closures on automated bag-making lines. FIBC loop webbing and valve assembly are integrated at this stage.

Auto bag-making lines Liner insertion FIBC loop assembly
6

Quality Inspection & Dispatch

Every batch undergoes final QC: GSM verification, tensile/elongation testing, dimension check, print adhesion, drop test, and visual 100% inspection. Test certificates accompany each consignment. Third-party inspection available.

100% visual QC Test certificates 3P inspection welcome

Why Integration Matters

Full Control = Consistent Quality

When a bag manufacturer sources fabric from one supplier, lamination from another and printing from a third, every process variation compounds. Quality drift is hard to trace and harder to fix.

At Rajaansh, the entire process chain is owned and operated in-house. If a denier specification drifts, we catch it at extrusion before it reaches the loom. If GSM is off, we correct loom parameters before lamination. No finger-pointing, no hidden variation, no surprises at your end.

This vertical integration also means faster turnaround — we don't wait on vendor delivery schedules to meet your timeline.
